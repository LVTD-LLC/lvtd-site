LVTD, LLC

Privacy Policy

Last updated June 12, 2026. This policy explains how LVTD, LLC handles information collected through lvtd.dev, service inquiries, payments, and client work.

Information Collected

LVTD collects information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, company, project details, technical context, files, credentials, and messages sent by email or other agreed channels.

The website may also collect basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, pages visited, timestamps, and server log data. The site stores your theme preference in local storage so the light or dark mode setting works across visits.

How Information Is Used

LVTD uses information to respond to inquiries, scope projects, provide services, process payments, operate hosted services, send project follow-up, maintain security, debug technical issues, improve the website, and comply with legal or tax obligations.

Payments

Payment details are handled by third-party payment processors such as Stripe. LVTD may receive payment status, customer contact details, invoice metadata, and transaction identifiers, but does not intentionally collect or store full payment card numbers on this website.

Analytics and Cookies

LVTD uses Plausible Analytics to understand website usage, file downloads, and outbound link activity. Plausible is designed to provide aggregate site metrics without building advertising profiles. The site may also use cookies or local storage where needed for security, payments, preferences, or third-party services.

Service Providers

LVTD may share information with service providers that help run the business, including hosting providers, email providers such as Mailgun, payment processors such as Stripe, analytics providers, code hosting tools, support tools, and infrastructure vendors. These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services to LVTD.

Client Data

For project work, LVTD may access systems, databases, files, logs, source code, and business data that you provide or authorize. LVTD uses that data to perform the requested work and does not sell client data. You should not provide sensitive personal data, regulated health data, payment card data, or other highly sensitive information unless it is necessary and expressly agreed in writing.

Retention

LVTD keeps information for as long as needed to provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations, and preserve useful project context. Information may be deleted or anonymized when it is no longer needed.

Security

LVTD uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No website, email system, hosting provider, or internet transmission is completely secure, so LVTD cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of personal information associated with you, subject to identity verification and legal retention requirements. You can also ask LVTD to stop using your information for non-essential communications.

International Processing

LVTD is based in the United States and may use service providers in the United States or other countries. By using the site or services, you understand that information may be processed in those locations.

Changes

LVTD may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last-updated date.

Contact

Privacy requests or questions can be sent to rasul@lvtd.dev.
